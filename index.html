About Me

Hello! I'm Carrie, a versatile professional with a diverse background in web development, instructional technology, and administration. With a recent education in full-stack development, I’ve gained hands-on experience in creating user-centered, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I am passionate about using technology to solve problems and improve user experiences, and I bring a detail-oriented approach to every project I undertake.

Prior to my journey in web development, I spent over a decade in administrative roles within the healthcare and educational sectors. During this time, I developed strong skills in project management, team collaboration, and adaptability—qualities that have enhanced my ability to work effectively in any environment. My experience includes everything from managing electronic health records to designing learning modules, which has given me a unique perspective on how technology can streamline processes and enhance learning.

I am driven by continuous learning and am always eager to take on new challenges, whether it is leading a team project, learning a new programming language, or volunteering in my community. As I continue to grow in the field of web development, I look forward to combining my technical skills with my background in communication and problem-solving to create impactful digital experiences
